SAI KIDS NEST place to learn, grow, share and succeed. Provides a caring and stimulating environment with bright spacious class rooms with variety of toys and learning materials. Dedicated playground with imported Play equipments. Motherly caring teachers focusing on holistic development of the children.











Vision & Mission


Vision: To provide a happy, caring and stimulating environment filled with unlimited opportunities where children will recognize and achieve their fullest potential, so that they can make their best contribution to society



Mission: To emphasize that each child is a different kind of flower and all together make this world into a beautiful garden.To make each child experience the joy of learning and provide encouragement to succeed in a safe environment. We will honor the small individual and nurture the potential of all children while making them believe in the beauty of their dreams.
